tinytalk

About The Project

TinyTalk is an AI-powered speech therapy assistant designed to help children overcome speech challenges through real-time analysis, personalized exercises, and gamified learning. We empower therapists with data-driven insights and make therapy engaging and accessible for kids and parents alike.

The system features a secure authentication system, real-time case management, and an intuitive interface that makes healthcare management more accessible and efficient for everyone involved.
